# NovaTel Customer Retention & Revenue Intelligence

## Business Report

---

# Executive Summary

This report presents the findings of a customer retention and revenue intelligence analysis conducted on a telecommunications dataset sourced from Maven Analytics. NovaTel is a fictional telecommunications company created for portfolio and business analysis purposes.

The objective of this analysis was to identify the primary drivers of customer churn, quantify revenue risk, evaluate customer loyalty patterns, and uncover opportunities to improve retention and long-term customer value.

Key findings revealed a customer churn rate of **26.54%**, with **3.68 Million** in revenue identified as being at risk. Competitor-related churn emerged as the largest customer loss driver, while Month-to-Month contracts and Fiber Optic customers represented the highest-risk segments. Long-tenure customers, referral-active customers, and customers adopting multiple services demonstrated significantly stronger retention and higher lifetime value.

The analysis identified several opportunities to improve customer retention, increase recurring revenue, and strengthen customer loyalty through targeted retention programs, contract migration initiatives, referral incentives, and service adoption strategies.

---

# Business Understanding

Customer retention is one of the most critical business challenges in the telecommunications industry. Customer acquisition costs are typically higher than customer retention costs, making churn reduction a strategic priority.

The business challenge addressed in this project was understanding:

* Why customers leave
* Which customer segments are most at risk
* How much revenue is being lost through churn
* Which services improve customer retention
* Which customer groups generate the highest long-term value

The analysis was designed to provide actionable insights that support data-driven retention and revenue optimization strategies.

# Strategic Recommendations

## Recommendation 1: Reduce Competitor-Driven Churn

Develop targeted retention campaigns for customers identified as high churn risk.

Actions:

* Competitive pricing reviews
* Loyalty incentives
* Personalized retention offers
* Customer win-back programs

---

## Recommendation 2: Increase Long-Term Contract Adoption

Encourage migration from Month-to-Month contracts to One-Year and Two-Year plans.

Actions:

* Contract upgrade discounts
* Loyalty rewards
* Bundle incentives

---

## Recommendation 3: Promote Retention-Driving Services

Increase adoption of:

* Online Security
* Premium Tech Support
* Device Protection Plans
* Online Backup

These services consistently correlate with lower churn rates.

---

## Recommendation 4: Expand Referral Programs

Create referral-based loyalty initiatives to encourage customer advocacy and improve retention.

---

## Recommendation 5: Prioritize High-Risk Segments

Focus retention efforts on:

* Fiber Optic customers
* Month-to-Month customers
* Senior customer groups
* Low-engagement customers

# Conclusion

This analysis identified customer churn, contract type, service adoption, referral activity, and customer tenure as the primary drivers of retention and revenue performance.

The findings indicate that NovaTel's largest opportunities lie in reducing competitor-driven churn, strengthening customer loyalty programs, increasing long-term contract adoption, and promoting retention-focused services.

By focusing on these areas, the organization can reduce revenue risk, improve customer lifetime value, and create a stronger foundation for long-term business growth.
